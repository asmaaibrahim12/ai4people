The PRESS Framework 5 principles at the heart of the book

A systematic approach ensuring every AI product you build embodies five essential principles — from first design decision through to long-term maintenance.

P

People-First Design

Build AI that amplifies human strengths rather than replacing them

R

Responsible at the Core

Embed ethics into every stage, from data collection to deployment

E

Explainability

Ensure AI decisions can be understood, trusted, and audited

S

Safety via Benchmarking

Rigorous evaluation before any system reaches real users

S

Sustainability

Design for lasting value — costs, infrastructure, and longevity
