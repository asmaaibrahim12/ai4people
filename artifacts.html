The Book

A Framework for Designing and Building Impactful AI-Powered Applications

Overview

Making AI Work for People empowers software engineers, product managers, and app designers to create AI-enabled applications that inspire trust, solve hard problems, and prioritize human needs.

Written by Asmaa Ibrahim, a product strategist who spent over a decade shipping AI-powered applications at Google and Vodafone before leaving to found her AI-native startup, Wildera Labs. This book shows you how to harness AI's transformative potential while guaranteeing applications remain ethical, accessible, and aligned with human values.

Publisher: Wiley
ISBN: 978-1394392094

The PRESS Framework

At the heart of the book is PRESS—a systematic approach ensuring AI products embody five essential principles

P

People-First Design

Strategies for building AI applications that amplify rather than replace human strengths and capabilities in real-world applications.

R

Responsible in the Core

Embed responsible AI practices into the foundation of your development process—from data collection through deployment.

E

Explainability & Interpretability

Ensure your AI systems are transparent and their decisions can be understood, trusted, and audited.

S

Safety via Benchmarking

Use rigorous evaluation methods and benchmarking to guarantee AI applications meet safety standards before reaching end users.

S

Sustainability & Long-Term Impact

Design for lasting value by managing costs, infrastructure challenges, and long-term maintenance requirements.
