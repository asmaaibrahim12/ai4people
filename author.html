Asmaa Ibrahim

Product Strategist · AI Author · Founder, Wildera Labs

Asmaa Ibrahim spent over a decade shipping AI-powered products at Google and Vodafone before leaving to found her AI-native startup, Wildera Labs. Her work sits at the intersection of human-centred design, responsible AI, and large-scale product delivery.

Her book, Making AI Work for People, distils that decade of experience into the PRESS Framework — a practical, reproducible approach for teams building AI products that are ethical, explainable, and built to last.
