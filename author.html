About the Author

Meet the person behind Making AI Work for People.

👤

The Author

The author of Making AI Work for People is a seasoned technology leader, researcher, and advocate for ethical AI. With over two decades of experience spanning technology, policy, and academia, they have been at the forefront of the movement to ensure AI systems serve the public good.

Their career has included leadership roles at major technology companies, advisory positions with government agencies on AI policy, and keynote speeches at international conferences on responsible AI development.

Driven by a deep conviction that technology should empower rather than marginalize, the author wrote this book to provide practitioners with the practical tools they need to build AI systems that are fair, transparent, and truly beneficial.
