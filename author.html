About the Author

Meet Asmaa Ibrahim—the mind behind Making AI Work for People.

👤

Asmaa Ibrahim

Asmaa Ibrahim is a product strategist with 15+ years of experience in high tech and over a decade designing and shipping AI-powered applications at companies including Google and Vodafone. She has established her first AI-native startup, Wildera Labs.

At Google, Asmaa led the Gemini in Firebase product launch and initiated a new partnership between Flutter and Vertex AI. She is a recognized Google Developer Expert, leveraging her AI expertise and cross-functional leadership across engineering, UX, and business strategy.

Asmaa received an award for her published paper at the ICICT2018 conference in the UK, which was selected as one of the top 10 based on content, quality, and presentation. She is also a published author on IEEE Xplore.
