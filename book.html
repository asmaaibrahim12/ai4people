About the Book

A comprehensive guide to building AI systems that prioritize human well-being, equity, and practical impact.

Overview

In an era where AI is transforming every facet of society, Making AI Work for People provides a clear, actionable roadmap for ensuring these powerful technologies truly serve human needs.

Drawing on real-world case studies, established ethical frameworks, and practical implementation guides, this book bridges the gap between AI theory and responsible practice. Whether you're building AI products, shaping policy, or studying the field, you'll find concrete tools to make a difference.

Each chapter includes hands-on artifacts—frameworks, checklists, templates, and assessment tools—that you can immediately apply in your work.
