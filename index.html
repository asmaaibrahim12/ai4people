Explore the Book The PRESS Framework

The PRESS Framework

A systematic approach ensuring AI products embody five essential principles

👥

People-First Design

Build AI applications that amplify human strengths and capabilities, keeping real user needs at the center of every product decision.

⚖

Responsible in the Core

Embed ethical principles and responsible practices into the foundation of AI development—not as an afterthought.

🔍

Explainability & Interpretability

Ensure AI systems are transparent and their decisions can be understood, trusted, and audited by the people they affect.

🛡

Safety via Benchmarking

Use rigorous evaluation and benchmarking to guarantee AI applications meet safety standards before reaching users.

🌱

Sustainability & Long-Term Impact

Design for lasting value—managing costs, infrastructure, and maintenance to ensure AI systems remain beneficial over time.
